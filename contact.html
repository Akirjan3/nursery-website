Our Location

Haslemere House Day Nursery is conveniently located in the heart of Mitcham, easily accessible by public transport and with parking available nearby. Our purpose-built facility provides a safe, secure, and stimulating environment for children aged 9 months to 5 years.

We are situated at 68 Haslemere Avenue, Mitcham, CR4 3BA, in a quiet residential area that offers the perfect balance of accessibility and tranquility for your child's early learning journey.
