We are Here to Support You

Parenting is a wonderful journey, but it's not always easy. At Haslemere House Day Nursery, we believe in supporting the whole family. We're here to provide guidance, resources, and a helping hand whenever you need it.

Comprehensive Family Support

We understand that every family is unique, with different needs, challenges, and circumstances. Our support services are designed to complement your parenting journey and provide you with the tools, resources, and confidence you need to help your child thrive.

From practical advice on child development to emotional support during difficult times, our experienced team is committed to being your partner in your child's early years. We believe that when families are supported, children flourish.

👶

Parenting Guidance

Expert advice on child development, behavior management, sleep routines, and age-appropriate activities to support your child at home.

🗣️

Communication Support

Strategies to enhance communication with your child, including language development tips and techniques for children with additional needs.

📚

Educational Resources

Home learning activities, recommended books and apps, and guidance on preparing your child for their next educational steps.

🤗

Emotional Wellbeing

Support for managing challenging behaviors, building resilience, and promoting positive mental health for the whole family.

🏠

Family Liaison

Regular check-ins with our family liaison team to discuss your child's progress and address any concerns or questions you may have.

🌐

Community Links

Connections to local services, support groups, and community resources that may benefit your family's specific needs.

⚠️ Emergency Support & Crisis Resources

If you need immediate support or are experiencing a family crisis, please reach out to these services:

Emergency Services: 999
Childline: 0800 1111
Families and Children's Hub: 020 8545 4226 / 4227

Our nursery team is also available for urgent support during opening hours: 020 8640 0822

Ready to Get Support?

Don't hesitate to reach out. We are here to help and support your family every step of the way.
