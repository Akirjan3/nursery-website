We are Here to Support You

Parenting is a wonderful journey, but it's not always easy. At Haslemere House Day Nursery, we believe in supporting the whole family. We're here to provide guidance, resources, and a helping hand whenever you need it.

Comprehensive Family Support

We understand that every family is unique, with different needs, challenges, and circumstances. Our support services are designed to complement your parenting journey and provide you with the tools, resources, and confidence you need to help your child thrive.

From practical advice on child development to emotional support during difficult times, our experienced team is committed to being your partner in your child's early years. We believe that when families are supported, children flourish.

Parent Groups & Workshops

We regularly organize parent support groups and workshops covering various topics relevant to early childhood development and parenting challenges. These sessions provide opportunities to connect with other parents, share experiences, and learn from experts.

Recent and upcoming topics include positive discipline strategies, supporting speech and language development, managing screen time, preparing for school transitions, and supporting children's emotional wellbeing. All parents and carers are welcome to attend these free sessions.
