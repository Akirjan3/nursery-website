Flexible Care Options for Every Family

At Haslemere House Day Nursery, we understand that every family has unique needs. That's why we offer flexible session options and competitive rates, with various funding opportunities available to support your childcare journey.

Perfect for working parents who need comprehensive childcare throughout the day.
  • Breakfast, lunch & afternoon snack included
  • Indoor & outdoor play activities
  • Educational activities & learning time

  • Regular progress updates

Government Funding Available

👶

2-Year-Old Funding

15 Hours Free

Available for eligible 2-year-olds from working families or those receiving certain benefits.

🧒

Universal 3 & 4-Year-Old

15 Hours Free

All 3 and 4-year-olds are entitled to 15 hours of free early education per week.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Working Parents

30 Hours Free

Extended funding for 3 & 4-year-olds of working parents, subject to eligibility criteria.

Tax-Free Childcare

Up to £2,000

Government scheme providing £2 for every £8 you pay towards childcare costs.

Please contact us to discuss your funding options and eligibility.
