Our Story

Founded in the heart of Mitcham, Haslemere House Day Nursery has been a cherished part of the local community for over 25 years. What began as a small initiative to support working families has blossomed into a vibrant, inclusive, and highly respected setting known for its caring team, outstanding education, and child-first ethos.

We are proud of our roots and continue to grow with the community—offering a safe and stimulating environment that meets the highest standards of care and learning.

What We Believe In

Child-Centred Learning

We follow the interests of each child, supporting their natural curiosity and promoting active learning.

Strong Relationships

We value strong, trusting relationships with children and families—laying the foundation for lifelong success.

Inclusive Practice

Every child is celebrated for who they are. We are an inclusive setting that supports all needs and backgrounds.

Outdoor Exploration

Our garden spaces and nature walks promote physical wellbeing, environmental awareness, and adventurous play.

Holistic Development

We nurture the whole child—emotionally, socially, physically, and intellectually—so they can grow in all areas.

Partnership with Parents

We view parents as key partners and actively involve them in their child’s early learning journey.
