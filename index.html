WELCOME TO HASLEMERE HOUSE DAY NURSERY!

At Haslemere House, we are committed to delivering high-quality early years education in line with the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework. We strive to create a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where every child can thrive, develop, and learn through play and exploration.

Our curriculum is carefully planned to support children's personal, social, and emotional development, communication and language skills, and physical growth, while also promoting literacy, mathematics, and understanding of the world.

We welcome children from 9 months to 5 years old, providing a safe, stimulating, and loving environment where they can learn, grow and thrive. Our nursery is open Monday to Friday from 8:00am to 6:00pm, 50 weeks of the year, to support busy families with flexible, dependable care.
