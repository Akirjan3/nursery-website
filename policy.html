Our Policy

CALL TODAY

Our nursery policies are designed to ensure the health, safety, and well-being of all children and staff. We are committed to providing a secure environment where children can learn and grow with confidence.

We follow strict safeguarding procedures to protect children from harm, and all staff are trained in child protection and first aid. Our health and safety policy covers cleanliness, food hygiene, and emergency procedures to maintain a safe setting at all times.

Inclusion and equality are central to our approach. We respect and celebrate diversity, ensuring that every child feels valued and supported regardless of background or ability.

We regularly review and update our policies to reflect current best practices and regulatory requirements, always aiming to provide the highest standard of care.
