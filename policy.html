Comprehensive Policies & Procedures

At Haslemere House Day Nursery, our comprehensive policy framework ensures the highest standards of care, safety, and education. We are committed to maintaining transparent, effective policies that protect and nurture every child in our care.

Our Policy Commitment

Our nursery policies are designed to ensure the health, safety, and well-being of all children and staff. We are committed to providing a secure environment where children can learn and grow with confidence, supported by clear guidelines and procedures that reflect best practices in early years education.

All our policies are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect current legislation, regulatory requirements, and evolving best practices in childcare and early years education. We maintain full compliance with Ofsted requirements and work closely with local authorities to ensure our standards remain exemplary.

🛡️

Safeguarding & Child Protection

Comprehensive procedures to protect children from harm, with trained staff following strict safeguarding protocols and multi-agency working practices.

🏥

Health & Safety

Rigorous health and safety standards covering cleanliness, food hygiene, emergency procedures, and maintaining a safe learning environment.

Inclusion & Equality

Celebrating diversity and ensuring every child feels valued and supported regardless of background, ability, or individual needs.

Specialised support for children with Special Educational Needs and Disabilities, ensuring inclusive practice and individual support plans.

Regulatory Compliance & Standards

We maintain full compliance with all regulatory requirements including Ofsted standards, local authority guidelines, and national early years legislation. Our policies are developed in line with the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework and statutory guidance for safeguarding children.

Regular training ensures all staff understand and implement our policies effectively. We conduct regular internal audits and welcome external inspections to maintain transparency and continuous improvement in our practices.

Questions About Our Policies?

We are happy to discuss any aspect of our policies and procedures. Our management team is available to answer questions and provide additional information.
