📰News & Events

Stay up to date with our latest nursery news, seasonal events, and important announcements. We host regular family days, workshops, and celebrations throughout the year to keep parents involved and informed about their child's development and learning journey.

📋Policies & Procedures

Our comprehensive policies are designed to ensure a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment. These include admissions, behavior management, health & safety, safeguarding, and confidentiality. All policies are regularly reviewed and updated.

🛡️Child Protection

Child safety is our highest priority. All staff are DBS-checked, trained in safeguarding, and adhere to strict protocols to protect children from harm. We work closely with local authorities to ensure best practice at all times.

🎓Staff Qualifications

Our team includes highly qualified childcare professionals with Level 2 and Level 3 Early Years qualifications. Staff participate in ongoing training to stay up to date with the latest childcare standards and best practices.

👥Staff-Child Ratios

We maintain strict staff-to-child ratios that exceed statutory requirements to ensure personalized attention, safety, and support for every child's development. This allows us to provide the individual care each child deserves.

🏥Health & Behavior

We promote healthy habits, balanced nutrition, and positive behavior management. Our dedicated staff work closely with parents to support individual health needs and encourage respectful social interactions among all children.

🔒Safety of Premises

Our nursery environment is regularly inspected and maintained to be secure, clean, and stimulating. Daily safety checks, secure access systems, and hygienic practices are integral to our routine operations.

🌈Equal Opportunities

We foster an inclusive community where every child and family is respected regardless of background, culture, or ability. Our curriculum celebrates diversity and promotes equality at all levels of our practice.
