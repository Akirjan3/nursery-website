Policies & Procedures

Our comprehensive policies are designed to ensure a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment. These include admissions, behaviour management, health & safety, safeguarding, and confidentiality. Please contact us for a full copy or to discuss any concerns.

Child safety is our highest priority. All staff are DBS-checked, trained in safeguarding, and adhere to strict protocols to protect children from harm. We work closely with local authorities to ensure best practice at all times.
